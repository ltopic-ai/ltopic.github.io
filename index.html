L-TOPIC

Long-horizon, decision-oriented AI platform

L-TOPIC builds an artificial intelligence platform for situations where single-shot answers are not enough: long-running projects, complex decisions, evolving contexts. The goal is to make intelligence operate over time, not only “at prompt level”.

Pragmatic approach: each capability is defined, observed, tested and improved (memory, planning, verification, traceability).

Vision & criteria → Concept & architecture

Positioning

The term “AGI” has no single scientific definition. Some actors define their own criteria and then claim AGI on that basis. Under such functional definitions, L-TOPIC could be considered “AGI-compatible”.

Our approach is different. L-TOPIC is positioned as a Project-Centric Intelligence (PCI), built on a Cognitive Architecture for Intelligence (CAI), with strong requirements for Controlled Cognitive Intelligence (CCI): coherence, governance, and traceability.

Key capabilities

Persistent memory

Structured, reusable memory: decisions, constraints, procedures, learnings — not just logs. Continuity across sessions and projects.

World model

An internal representation of context (actors, rules, risks, hypotheses), continuously updated over time.

Long-horizon multi-goal reasoning

Decomposition, prioritization, arbitration and replanning across long trajectories, without losing continuity.
