L-TOPIC

Functional AGI-like agentic system

L-TOPIC is an agentic architecture designed to plan, execute, and optimize complex, multi-objective, long-running missions using verification protocols, structured memory, and experience-driven learning.

Status: Design & prototyping. Some capabilities described here are under development and validation.

What L-TOPIC does

  • Hierarchical planning across goals and sub-tasks
  • Multi-objective decision-making
  • Verification-first execution workflows
  • Structured long-term memory
  • Learning from operational feedback

Position on AGI

L-TOPIC does not claim to be an Artificial General Intelligence. We use the term functional AGI-like to describe a system focused on observable, testable capabilities rather than philosophical claims.
