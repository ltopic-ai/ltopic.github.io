L-TOPIC

L-TOPIC is a research-driven cognitive architecture designed to explore long-term, goal-oriented artificial intelligence systems.

The project focuses on persistent memory, internal world models, and structured reasoning across extended time horizons.

  • Persistent memory beyond single interactions
  • Internal world modeling and state representation
  • Multi-goal, long-horizon reasoning

L-TOPIC does not claim to be an Artificial General Intelligence. It explores AGI-like functional properties through a rigorous, incremental, and research-oriented approach.
